Ghanchi Vivah - Frequently Asked Questions

1️⃣ Why can’t I see detailed profiles?

Only upgraded members can view complete profile information. Free users can view only profile highlights.

2️⃣ What is a mutual connection?

A mutual connection is created when two users show interest in each other. Only mutual connections are allowed to chat.

3️⃣ What features are included in the upgrade?

Premium upgrade gives access to:
✔ View complete profile details
✔ Like & Show interest
✔ Chat with mutually connected users

4️⃣ How do I delete my account?

You can delete your profile anytime from the app under Profile → Delete Account. All associated data will also be deleted.

5️⃣ Who can use Ghanchi Vivah?

This app is designed to help members of the Ghanchi community find suitable life partners.

6️⃣ Is my data safe?

Yes. We do not sell or misuse your data. Your information is used only to match suitable profiles as per our Privacy Policy.

7️⃣ How do I contact support?

You can reach us anytime at ghanchivivah@gmail.com.
